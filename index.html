A modern publishing plataform

Grow your audience and build your online brand

Designed for the future

Introducing an extensible editor

Blogr geatures an execeedingly intuitive interface which lets you focus on one thing: creating content. the editor supports management of multiple blogs and allows easy manipulation of embeds such as images, videos, and Markdown. Extensibility with puglins and themes provide easy ways to add funcionality or change the looks of a blog.

Robust content management

Flexible content management enebles users to easily move through posts. Increase the usability of your blog by adding customized categories, sections, format or flow. With this funcionality, you're in full control.

State of the art infrastruture

With the reliability and speed in mind, worldride data centers provide the backbone for ultra-fast conectivity. This ensures your site will load instantly, no matter where you readers are, keeping your site competitive.

Free, open , simple

Blogr is a free and open source application backed by a large comunity of helpful developers. It supports features such as code syntax highlighting, RSS feeds, social media integration, third-party commenting tools, and works seamlessy with Google Analytics. The architecture is clean and is relatively easy to learn.

Powerful tooling

Batteries included. We built a simple and straightforward CLI tool that makes customization and deployment a breeze, but capable of producing even the most complicated sites.
